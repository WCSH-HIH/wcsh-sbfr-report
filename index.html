System Bottleneck Focused Reform (SBFR)

SBFR is a quality improvement initiative aimed at identifying and resolving operational bottlenecks in healthcare delivery. Through structured nighttime assessments across administrative, clinical, and non-clinical areas, it enables multidisciplinary teams to improve processes, enhance patient care, and ensure continuous monitoring and sustainable operational improvements.
